Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect
    The Bot stores only the data required to operate its leveling and cosmetic systems. This includes:
    - Discord User ID
    - XP amount
    - Level
    - Rebirth count
    - XP multiplier
    - Daily message count
    - Cosmetics and badges unlocked
    - Timestamps related to XP limits
No other personal information is collected.

2. How We Use the Data
    Data is used exclusively to:
    - Track XP and levels
    - Enforce daily XP caps
    - Manage cosmetics and badges
    - Handle rebirth progression
    - Display leaderboards
We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage
    Data is stored securely in the Bot’s database or file system. Only the Bot owner has access to this data.

4. Data Removal
    Users may request deletion of their stored data by contacting the Bot owner. Deleting your data will reset your progress.

5. Children’s Privacy
    The Bot does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users must follow Discord’s age requirements.

6. Changes to This Policy
    This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot means you accept the updated policy.
